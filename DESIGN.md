# Tester — Design Guidelines

## Brand & Color
- Orange-anchored, energetic, commerce-oriented palette (Swiggy/Zomato references)
- Primary: vibrant orange (#FF6B00 family) for CTAs, active nav, highlights
- Neutrals: clean whites and light grays for surfaces; near-black for primary text
- Semantic: green (success/order complete), red (reject/suspend), amber (pending approval/active orders)
- Generous whitespace; structural clarity over decoration

## Typography
- Modern sans-serif system stack
- Clear hierarchy: bold large headers, medium section titles, regular body, muted captions for metadata (prices, ratings, timestamps)
- Prices and KPI numbers emphasized with weight

## Layout & Components
- Mobile-first, responsive
- Card-based shop and product listings with image, name, rating, price, meta
- Promotional banner slider (carousel) on customer home
- Horizontal-scroll category chips and featured-shop cards
- Vertical card feed for recommended products
- Fixed top search bar on home
- Bottom navigation bar (customer app): Home · Search · Orders · Dashboard · Profile — orange active state
- KPI/stat cards for Shop Owner and Admin dashboards (Total Orders, Total Revenue, Active Products, Customer Reviews)
- Status badges/pills for order lifecycle (pending, accepted, preparing, completed, rejected) and approval states

## Interaction & Feel
- Fast, snappy, optimized images, fast loading
- Real-time order status updates with clear visual progression
- Clear empty states for cart, orders, notifications
- Professional, trustworthy tone in all UI copy

## Elevation & Surfaces
- Subtle shadows on cards and the bottom nav
- Rounded corners on cards, buttons, inputs, and badges
- Clear separation between primary actions (filled orange) and secondary actions (outline/ghost)
